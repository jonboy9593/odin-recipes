Cheeseburger Recipe

Try this gourmet smash burger with sauteed mushrooms, Swiss cheese, and homemade truffle mayonnaise next time you're in a burger mood.

Its easy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1/2c Mayo
  • 2tsp Lemon Juice
  • 3/4tsp Truffle Oil
  • 1tsp Chopped Parsley
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1 1/4tsp Salt
  • 1/8tsp + 1/2tsp Black Pepper
  • 3tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1 16oz Package Cremini Mushrooms
  • 5 Sprigs Thyme
  • 1tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/4tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4tsp Onion Powder
  • 4 Slices Swiss Cheese
  • 4 Kaiser Rolls; Split & Toasted
